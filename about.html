About the Innovative Visionaries Club

The Innovative Visionaries Club (IV Club) is a dynamic community of innovators, changemakers, and leaders committed to transforming lives and creating a better tomorrow. Founded in 2021, our mission is to inspire young minds to dream big, take initiative, and drive impactful change in their communities and beyond.

Through workshops, summits, mentorship, and groundbreaking initiatives, IV Club nurtures the next generation of visionaries who are committed to transforming ideas into real-world solutions.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

To empower and nurture young people with the skills, resources, and opportunities to innovate, lead, and make a difference in the world.

Vision

To be a global force of young leaders transforming lives and pioneering solutions for a better tomorrow.

Meet Our President

Amantle Hazel Motsamai

President, Innovative Visionaries

Amantle Hazel Motsamai is a dynamic and purpose-driven leader dedicated to empowering communities, fostering innovation, and leading with integrity. As the President of Innovative Visionaries, she champions bold initiatives that inspire creativity, self-reliance, and transformation among youth.

She also leads the BAC Wealth Builders Club, a platform cultivating financial literacy and investment knowledge in young people. She is pursuing a Professional Bachelor’s Degree in Insurance and is a mentee in the Future Leaders Program.

Her entrepreneurial pursuits include Luxe_Vibe, and she’s deeply passionate about fashion, faith, investment, and personal development—all grounded in strong Christian values.

Notable Engagements:

  • Attendee – RAMI Millionaire Club, 2024
