Our Organizations Future Plans

Expanding our reach to impact more students across Africa

We aim to scale our programs, partnerships, and outreach to engage students from across the African continent. This involves collaborating with regional institutions, launching pan-African initiatives, and leveraging digital platforms to ensure more youth can access our resources, mentorship, and innovation-driven opportunities.

ReLaunching the Youthnnovation Hackathon Challenge in 2025

In late 2025, we're bringing back the Youthnnovation Hackathon Challenge with a renewed focus on solving real-world issues faced by youth globally. Participants will use cutting-edge technology and creative thinking to develop impactful solutions aligned with themes such as education, health, unemployment, and climate change. The challenge aims to empower youth as problem-solvers and changemakers.

Establishing an alumni network to continue the legacy of innovation and impact

We're creating an alumni network to maintain lasting connections with past members and amplify our community's influence. This network will offer mentorship, collaboration opportunities, and support systems for emerging leaders, ensuring that the spirit of innovation continues to thrive beyond graduation.

Advancing our alignment with SDGs to further promote inclusive education, gender equality, and climate action

Our initiatives will increasingly reflect and support the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We will integrate inclusive education, gender equity, and environmental consciousness into all our programs, ensuring that the impact we create is both socially responsible and globally relevant.

We're also launching new hackathons and strengthening our alignment with global goals and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
We aim to align ourselves very much with Confidence Building Measures and Cybersecurity Policies.

Stay Informed

The Innovative Visionaries Club (IV Club) is committed to transforming lives and creating a better tomorrow. Founded in 2021, its mission is to inspire young minds to dream big, take initiative, and drive impactful change in their communities and beyond.
