Our Initiatives

Career & Business Talk Series

This impactful initiative bridges the gap between academia and the professional world. Through sessions with experienced professionals, participants gain insight into various career paths, business strategies, and entrepreneurial development.
• Highlight: A recent session with Goitsemang J. Khutshafalo was well-received, offering valuable guidance on career advancement and business growth.
• Impact: Over 100 attendees reported greater clarity in navigating their career and entrepreneurial goals.
• Ongoing Focus: Equipping young people with actionable advice to thrive in competitive markets.

Leadership with Vision Summit

This flagship annual event provides a platform for youth leaders to redefine leadership by promoting transformation, sustainability, and inclusivity. 2023 Theme: Leadership Redefined: Maximize & Expand Your Impact.
• Workshops and Panels: Becoming Agents of Change, Building Sustainable Enterprises, Dominating as Female Leaders in Leadership.
• Outcome: Attendees reported a 95% increase in their understanding of leadership dynamics and sustainable practices.
• Collaborators: Botswana Accountancy College, Gamer’s Territory, Tshepiso Malema Speaks, and other thought leaders.

Visionary Hour Podcast

A new and exciting addition, the Visionary Hour Podcast creates a platform for growth, development, and entertainment.
• Objective: Students interview professionals, entrepreneurs, and influencers to gain insights into their journeys, ideas for growth, and valuable life lessons.
• Format: A mix of edutainment and inspiration.
• Impact Goals: Promote continuous learning, strengthen communication skills, and encourage sharing of innovative ideas.

Cyber 9/12 Strategy Challenge

• Objective: Compete in a prestigious international cybersecurity and policy competition.
• Success: Represented Botswana at the Cyber 9/12 Strategy Challenge in Cape Town, South Africa. Placed 7th out of top Southern African institutions.
• Highlight: Showcased the club’s capability in cybersecurity and digital strategy.

North Digital Transformation Hackathon

• Objective: Promote digital transformation through innovation.
• Success: Partnered with BITC, Limegig, and Tribease. Hosted 200+ participants, 5 prototypes advanced to regional competitions.
• Highlight: Featured in discussions on youth-led innovation.

The Innovative Visionaries Club (IV Club) is committed to transforming lives and creating a better tomorrow. Founded in 2021, its mission is to inspire young minds to dream big, take initiative, and drive impactful change in their communities and beyond.
